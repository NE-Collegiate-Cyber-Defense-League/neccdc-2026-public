## Security in your environment

**Firewalls**  
Your internal environment connects back into ChefOps core for secure monitoring and operations.
Only authorized admins can manage them, and access is limited by role (e.g. network admins, system admins, IT).

**Secure Access**  
A private network link (VPN) connects corporate and branch sites so staff can work securely from different locations. Traffic over this link is protected.

**Secure Sign-in**  
You get a single, secure way to sign in to the systems and apps in your environment. Access is tied to roles so people only see what they’re allowed to use.

**Protection on your Systems**  
- Logins are protected against brute-force and repeated failed attempts.  
- Activity on your servers is monitored for unusual behavior so issues can be caught early.
- End to end encreyption is utlizaed to protect your most sensitive sause information.

**Documentation**  
Customers are given access to documentation and a place to share internal code. You access it through a web interface with your normal sign-in. This is where customer docs, backup info, and other shared content live.

**Automation**  
Scheduled and on-demand tasks run in your environment (e.g. backups, updates, checks). Who can run or change these is controlled by RBAC.

**Monitoring and Alerts**  
Dashboards and alerts show how your systems are doing. You can get notified when something needs attention.

**Backups**  
Important data in your environment is backed up on a schedule. That includes things like firewall configuration and POS/data where applicable. How long we keep backups and how often they run is dependant on your contract.
